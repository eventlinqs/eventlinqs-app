Overview

Sounds On Sunday returns to the hallowed grounds of the Greenwood in North Sydney with all your favourite DJ's, MC's and more.

27 Years ago on the June long weekend, Sounds On Sunday was born.

SOS at The Greenwood in North Sydney became the place to be on a Sunday.

Running every Sunday for 10 years, and with a rotating roster of over 50 of Sydney and the east coast's finest DJ's and artists across 3 arenas and various genres of music, Sounds became a proper institution.

This June long weekend, we reunite.

We come together to see all the old faces and all our favourite artists in one of the

best venues Sydney has to offer.

We come together for the love of music,

and to dance our arses off one more time.

Venue: The Greenwood Hotel, North Sydney

Date: Sunday 7th June 2026

Time: 1pm to 10:30pm
