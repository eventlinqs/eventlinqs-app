ANALYST NOTE: Humanitix runs the tightest, most confident pricing page in the set. Differentiators: (1) The price IS the story - "$Nothing. Nada. zero." for free events is written with personality, not legalese. (2) Flat, all-feature pricing: "ALL plans get access to ALL features" removes the feature-gating anxiety every other platform creates. (3) The charity mission ("100% of profits go to charity", "turn booking fees into a force for good") is the moral differentiator that reframes the fee from a cost into a contribution - emotionally disarming the single biggest objection in ticketing (fees feel like greed). (4) A dedicated Charities & Schools tier (2.5% + $0.50) signals values alignment, not just discounting. (5) Extremely short page - confidence through brevity; they do not over-justify. Weakness: very little scale/volume proof, no named testimonials on this page, thin trust scaffolding beyond the mission claim - they are betting the mission carries it. AU-specific: AUD pricing, "excl. GST" noted, /au/ locale.
