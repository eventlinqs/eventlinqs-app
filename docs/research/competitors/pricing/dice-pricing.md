---

ANALYST NOTE: DICE's positioning is a single sharp ideological claim - "the only ticketing platform that puts the fan first" - repeated as the spine of the page. No price, no percentage, no free-tier statement, no payout terms anywhere. Trust is built through (a) a named operator testimonial, (b) recognisable artist case studies, and (c) a values narrative. The entire page is a funnel into a heavily-segmented sales qualification form; DICE deliberately treats pricing as a relationship conversation, not a published number. The fan-first ideology is the differentiator they lead with before any feature or cost. Notably, DICE's consumer model (no separate booking fee shown to fans; fee absorbed into face value) is implied by the "fan first" framing but never stated on this organiser page.
