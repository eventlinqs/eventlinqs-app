# Content plan - aligned to the certified platform (2026-07-11)

Growth lever 4 (building in public and content), serving lever 1 (supply-side
recruitment). Lead hooks everywhere: THE EVENT LAUNCH KIT and MAGIC START.
Switch triggers: SEATING for seated venues, PROOF-OF-DRAW for performers.
Every piece routes to a concierge conversation or the waitlist. Channels: the
5 socials (LinkedIn, Facebook, Instagram, X, TikTok) scheduled through
Buffer; YouTube for build-in-public longform; shorts cut from everything.
Script framework: Hook, Point, Proof, Punch.

## The one-line positioning (use verbatim)

- Platform: "Build your event, map your room, get your complete promo kit,
  in minutes. Free."
- Artists: "Instagram is where artists get seen. EventLinqs is where they
  get booked and paid."
- Ownership: "Your attendees are yours. Export them any time, no walls."

## Claims discipline (binding on every post)

Everything in OUTREACH-TEMPLATES.md applies: no competitor names, no
exclamation marks, Australian English, community-first language. We can say
we are faster to publish, deeper on seating, the only ones showing
performers their draw, and that a ticket can never be scanned twice. We do
not claim cheapest all-in, recurring events, wallet passes, email campaigns,
or offline scanning. The gig board and performer directory do not exist in
public until the founder flips them; the planned reveal is the
"performers, bring your numbers" moment, post-launch.

## Content pillars (map every piece to one)

1. **THE KIT (hero pillar).** The moment of publish: live page, QR poster,
   invitation card, tracked links, reach panel, one screen. Show it, do not
   describe it.
2. **MAGIC START (the wow clip).** Speak a sentence, watch the draft build,
   publish inside a minute. The single most repostable asset we have.
3. **THE ROOM (seated venues).** Seat map timelapses, whole-table booking in
   one tap, moving an attendee and the email landing, buyers moving
   themselves. Ends on: seating is optional, but when you need it, it is all
   there.
4. **PROOF OF DRAW (performers).** A performer shares their tracked link and
   the sale lands on their dashboard. Teases the artist layer WITHOUT the
   gig board.
5. **THE PROMISE (trust).** All-in pricing on the first screen, free events
   free, your attendee list exportable. Show the live payout calculator.
6. **THE TERRITORY (Geelong plus Melbourne as one).** One crowd, an hour
   apart: venue spotlights, organiser stories, launch-week beats. Every
   other city: waitlist drumbeats ("[city], put your hand up").
7. **BUILD IN PUBLIC (founder channel).** One video per shipped milestone,
   honest numbers, cut into shorts.

| Day | Piece | Pillar | CTA |

Weekly newsletter (Resend/Loops, transactional-clean list only): what is on
in the territory this week plus one organiser story. No purchased lists, no
cold email blasts; outbound stays personal per OUTREACH-TEMPLATES.md.

## Reserved beats (do not spend early)

- **"Performers, bring your numbers."** The gig board and directory
  switch-on. Assets to prepare quietly: directory walkthrough, gig post to
  booked-and-paid story arc. Publish NOTHING until the founder flips
  gig_board and artist_showcase.
- **First sold-out room.** The organiser tells it; we repost.
- **First city unlocked by its waitlist.** The waitlist counter story.

## Measurement (honest numbers only)

Per piece: profile taps and link clicks (tracked links make every post
attributable); per week: concierge calls booked, organisers onboarded,
waitlist joins by city; monthly: which pillar drives onboarded organisers,
double down there. Real platform numbers only in public claims, never
projections.
